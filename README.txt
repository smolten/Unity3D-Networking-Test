A project used to test the networking/ multiplayer capabilities of Unity.

Based on instructions/ information from:
https://unity3d.com/learn/tutorials/topics/multiplayer-networking/introduction-simple-multiplayer-example?playlist=29690

To use:
From Unity, Ctrl-B to build and then launch a standalone application, and then launch a 2nd from within the editor. Choose the "Host" option from one client, and the "Client" option from the other client.
